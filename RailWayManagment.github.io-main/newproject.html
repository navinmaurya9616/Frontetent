Ticket Cancellation

1:- 5% of the ticket price will be cut during cancellation as a fine. It has been written in the rules which you agree while booking a ticket!!!!!!

2:- Ticket cancelling will be applicable only in case valid mobile number has been given at the time of booking.

3:- Online cancellation shall be permitted only upto 30 minutes before the scheduled departure of the train if ticket is RAC/Waitlist.

4:- Once the passenger confirms full cancellation of PNR will be done and PNR will be marked as Cancelled but not refunded in the system. Seat/berth will be released. Refund amount due will also be displayed to the passenger on website
